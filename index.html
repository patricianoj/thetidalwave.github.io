About Us

Who Are We?

Antonio Martinez, editor, and Patricia Ornelas, web developer, are the creators of this webpage. Joined by the writers, photographers, and journalists of Making Waves Academy, we coommunicate the latest news to the public. We are journalists who want to know the truth and expose it.

We are a student independent website based in Richmond, California. We aim to create an independent student voice without restrictions from our school. By doing so, we plan to avoid the censorship that may come from controversial topics that need to be discussed.

Censorship causes people to be unaware and uninformed of important issues that exist within our community. We seek to promote conversation in order to help fix issues within our community. By creating a safe place for students, staff, and parents, we hope to bring light to issues and concerns that might not be brought up otherwise.
